# Roadmap Builder

> **Status: planned.** The spec below is drafted; implementation is coming. Want it sooner, or have a use case? Open an issue.

Turn a pile of initiatives into a clear **Now / Next / Later** roadmap that sets direction without committing to false precision.

## When to use this skill

- Translating a backlog of ideas into a communicable roadmap
- Aligning stakeholders on sequence and intent
- Replacing a date-driven Gantt with an outcome-driven view

## Inputs to ask for

1. The list of initiatives or themes.
2. Strategic priorities or goals they should ladder up to.
3. Known constraints — dependencies, capacity, hard deadlines.

## Output (planned)

A Now / Next / Later roadmap where each item carries the outcome it drives, its rationale for placement, and any dependency that gates it — plus a short narrative a leader can read aloud.

## Operating principles (planned)

- Sequence by outcome and dependency, not by wish.
- "Later" is a real, respectable place — not a graveyard.
- Every item states *why now* (or why not yet).
