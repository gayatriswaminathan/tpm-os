# Meeting Orchestrator

> **Status: planned.** The spec below is drafted; implementation is coming. Have a use case? Open an issue.

Make meetings earn their hour — a focused agenda going in, and clear decisions and actions coming out.

## When to use this skill

- Preparing an agenda for a steering review, working session, or standup
- Turning messy meeting notes into decisions and action items
- Producing a crisp follow-up that survives contact with reality

## Inputs to ask for

1. Meeting purpose, attendees, and time available.
2. For prep: the topics or decisions needed.
3. For follow-up: the raw notes or transcript.

## Output (planned)

Before: a time-boxed agenda with a clear objective per item and a decision called out where one is needed. After: a summary with decisions made, action items (owner + date), and open questions carried forward.

## Operating principles (planned)

- Every agenda item has an objective; every meeting has a purpose.
- Separate decisions from discussion from FYI.
- An action item without an owner and a date isn't an action item.
